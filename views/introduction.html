What is Tracker?

Tracker is the Ondina Issue tracking system. This is where you can report bugs in the Ondina Control Panel and suggest new features. Your participation helps making Ondina better.

How do I report a bug?

Firstly, identify the section of the control panel that is affected. You can simply include the whole URL of the page in your report:

Browse other issues before posting your own. A few hints can help us solve your issue quickly and without needing to contact you again:

  • What did you expect tp happen?
  • What did actually happen?
And, if possible:
  • What is supposed to happen?

Remember: If you post more detail in your report, it will help us resolve the issue more quickly.

What if my issue contains sensitive information?

If you click Mark as security issue, it will be only be shown to Ondina employees.
